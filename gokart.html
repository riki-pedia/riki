Go Kart Racing

Go kart racing is a sport for children (mostly). It is played by driving a racing kart around and trying to be the fastest. It is kind of like training to be in NASCAR, a competitive, major league variation of go kart racing. There are many places you can do it, including a local place in Reno called Need 2 Speed. There are many more and to find them, just Google "go kart racing near me".
